03 / History

A conflict
in cycles

Every escalation has left Gaza further behind. Each operation built on the destruction of the last.

Summer 2006
Operation Summer Rains + Autumn Clouds
Gaza Beach attack — 8 civilians killed. 4-month operation followed by 1-week operation.
2008–2009
Operation Cast Lead
1,400+ Palestinians killed. White phosphorus used on civilian areas. First ground invasion since 2005.
2012
Operation Pillar of Defense
158 Palestinians killed across two operations.
2014
Operation Protective Edge
2,200+ killed, 10,000+ injured. 18,000 homes destroyed. Battle of Shuja'iyya — 120 killed in a single neighbourhood. Rubble: 2.5 million tonnes.
2018–2021
Great March of Return + Operation Guardian of the Walls
223 killed in border protests. 8,000+ with live-ammunition disabilities. 1,042 housing units destroyed in May 2021 alone. Samir Mansour bookstore — 100,000 books destroyed.
Oct 2023 — Present
Current conflict
73,188+ confirmed killed. 90% of population displaced. 92% of housing damaged or destroyed. Ongoing as of March 2026.
